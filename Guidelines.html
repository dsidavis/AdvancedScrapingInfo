Some very general organization principles to follow:

  1. Work in small-ish groups and develop a strategy for the URL you are scraping.
  2. Get the data in whatever way is most convenient. Elegance and generality are not the important criteria here.
  3. Ask for advice, technical assistance, etc. at any time!
  4. See here for workflow and structure of the resulting data
  5. Record what you do, ideally in code, so that you and others can reproduce and verify the data.
  6. Verify that the data you obtain is the same as it is on the Web site.
  7. Save/archive both the data and the HTML documents (and the related files, i.e., the JavaScript, CSS, etc.) Use "Save As" in your browser.
